Important Nutrition Tips

  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables every day to get essential nutrients.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Limit processed foods and opt for whole foods as much as possible.
  • Maintain a balanced diet with the right proportions of protein, carbs, and healthy fats.
  • Don't skip meals—especially breakfast, as it helps to kick-start your metabolism.
  • Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating.
  • Plan meals in advance to ensure a consistent and healthy eating routine.
